ABOUT US

Who we are...

Fussilat Training and Skilling Center (FTSC) is a leading institute that offers quality education and training in various fields since 2022. FTSC aims to develop professional human resources who can excel in their careers and contribute to the society.
FTSC offers courses in hospitality, arts, leadership, and entrepreneurship, among others. FTSC follows the world-renowned Australian Model of Competency Based Training (CBT), which ensures that the students not only acquire knowledge, but also develop skills and attitudes that enable them to perform effectively in their chosen fields.
FTSC has received international recognition and awards for its excellence in education and training. FTSC has also provided corporate training to many national and transnational organizations, enhancing their human capital and productivity. FTSC is more than just an institute; it is a community of learners, innovators, and leaders who are ready to make a positive impact in the world.
